# Problem Statement Selected  

HR Cost Intelligence Engine

# Project Description

Navytics AI is an AI-powered HR Cost Intelligence platform that helps organizations understand how employee time and meeting activities contribute to project costs.

Organizations conduct numerous meetings daily, but often lack visibility into which projects consume employee resources and HR expenditure. Navytics AI analyzes meeting data, attributes meetings to relevant projects, estimates meeting costs, and presents actionable insights through interactive dashboards.

The platform is designed for HR teams, project managers, finance teams, and business leaders who want better visibility into resource utilization and project spending.

# Approach

I started by understanding the core problem: organizations struggle to identify which projects are consuming employee time through meetings and how much those meetings cost.

I designed a workflow where meeting data is imported, analyzed, assigned to projects, costed, and visualized.

The solution consists of:

* Calendar Import Center
* AI-Powered Meeting Attribution
* Meeting Intelligence Engine
* Cost Simulator
* Executive Dashboard

AI is used to simulate project attribution by analyzing meeting context, attendee patterns, keywords, and historical project associations. The platform also provides explainable AI reasoning and confidence scores for every attribution decision.

The focus was on creating a practical and intuitive user experience while demonstrating the complete HR cost intelligence workflow.
